use it later

The PrepaidGas protocol facilitates the Gas futures market, enabling individuals to buy Gas in advance and trade or use it when needed in the future.

How it works?

  1. A claimer initiates an order, specifying the required amount of Gas, its accessibility timeframe, and the proposed Gas price in any token.
  2. An independent executor voluntarily agrees to process any meta transaction upon request and commits a certain guarantee as collateral.
  3. Individuals holding ERC1155 tokens associated with the Gas reserves have the option to sign the transaction and request the executor to execute it.
  4. If the executor declines to process the transaction:
    1. A liquidator can step in, execute the transaction, and claim the locked reward along with the executor's collateral.
    2. Gas token holders may choose to disregard the transaction, still securing the executor's collateral.
Each order is encapsulated as an individual ERC-1155 token, which can be divided and transferred independently.

Reward distribution

The project's lifecycle can be divided into two distinct stages: pre-token distribution (Stage 1) and post-token distribution (Stage 2).

Stage 1
  • 80% of rewards are channeled into the development team;
  • 20% is redirected to a public funding pool, such as Gitcoin or an alternative.
Stage 2
  • 80% of the reward pool is distributed among validators;
  • 10% of reward tokens are set aside for the developers;
  • 10% is contributed to the public funding pool.
